# 07 — Startup Credits (free money for your AWS bill)

**Goal:** apply for AWS credits so your early infrastructure is effectively free.
**Time:** ~15 minutes to apply.

AWS runs a program called **AWS Activate** for startups. Eligible companies can receive **AWS credits** (commonly up to **$1,000** self-service, and up to **$100,000** through partner/accelerator tiers), plus discounts and support.

## How to apply
1. Search the web for **"AWS Activate"** and go to the official AWS Activate page.
2. Choose the tier that fits:
   - **Activate Founders** — for self-funded/early startups (no investor required).
   - **Activate Portfolio** — larger credits if you're tied to a partnered VC, accelerator, incubator, or startup program.
3. Fill out the application with your company details. You typically need:
   - A company name and website (even a simple landing page helps).
   - A brief description of what you're building.
   - Your AWS account ID.

## Tips
- Having a basic **company entity** (even an LLC) and a **website** improves your odds and unlocks higher tiers.
- Joining a recognized **accelerator or startup program** can qualify you for the larger Portfolio credits.
- Credits usually have an expiration window, so apply around when you'll actually start spending.

> Note: exact amounts, tiers, and eligibility change over time. Confirm current details on the official AWS Activate page before relying on a specific number.

### ✅ Done when
- You've applied to AWS Activate (or noted the requirements you still need, like a website or entity).

That's the foundation. From here, you build your product on top of it.
